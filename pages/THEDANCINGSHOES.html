The Dancing Shoes

SYNOPSIS

Twelve princesses mysteriously wear out their shoes every night, despite being locked in their room. The king, puzzled by this, promises a reward to anyone who can uncover the secret. A soldier discovers the princesses' secret and must outwit them to reveal the truth behind their nightly escapades. The story blends mystery, magic, and cleverness in an enchanting tale of hidden worlds.
